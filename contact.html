Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our programs, volunteering, and how to get involved.

We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds! You can get involved through our mentorship programs, community outreach activities, or skills development workshops. Contact us to discuss opportunities that match your interests and availability.

We currently serve 75 communities across 8 counties in Kenya, including Nairobi, Kiambu, Machakos, and Nakuru. Our programs reach both urban and rural areas, with a focus on underserved communities.

Your donations directly support our three core programs: Youth Mentorship (45%), Community Outreach (35%), and Skills Development (20%). We maintain full transparency with quarterly impact reports showing exactly how funds are utilized.

Absolutely! We actively seek partnerships with businesses, NGOs, government agencies, and educational institutions. Partnership opportunities include program sponsorship, skill-sharing, resource provision, and collaborative community projects.

Program applications are accepted year-round through our community coordinators or by contacting us directly. We assess applications based on need, commitment, and program capacity. Priority is given to individuals from underserved communities.
